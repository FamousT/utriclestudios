The Mavians


The Mavians is an ancient organisation bent on discovering the ultimate power to control everything and everyone in the world, finds this power source in the centre of the African Continent. Further studies by them showed that the only way for them to access that power was to get specific totems that represent the entire countries in the African Continent.



The Adventures of Adama


he Adventures of Adama is a spinoff of the character, Adama in Gammy and the Living Things. Often mistaken for a scavenging rat, Adama is a forest shrew who knows how to drum and is also a member of Gammy’s Band.

His real identity as an Adamawa Mountain Shrew is revealed when he deploys some of his martial arts skills in saving the band from danger. Adama is involved in so many adventures which are chronicled in this entertaining series

Cluckstars

Our First Tv Series

Cluckstars is the story of five local chickens who are always up to a new adventure which provides an opportunity for them to learn. It is a curriculum based numeracy show that will keep our young learners engaged and help them sharpen their numeracy skills and other key skills such as listening, team work, leadership and respect. Each episode has a theme and specific learning objectives.

Join the Cluckstars on an adventure of problem solving, learning and adventure.
