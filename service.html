WHAT WE DO


We tell compelling African stories, crafting ideas into magic and meaning with our top-of-the-line team of animators, illustrators and visual production experts. Our expertise ranges from visual production, post-production, 2D/3D animation, motion graphics, documentaries, illustration to visual effects creation.

2D & 3D ANIMATION

Our proficiency in 2D traditional Animation cuts across the building of characters, creatures, FX, and backgrounds in the two-dimensional space, helping you tell your story in a compelling and visually-appealing way.

From hyper-realistic digital environments using storytelling mediums like video games, television and filmmaking to well-designed life-like characters, we optimize and bring to life three-dimensional imagery to tell better stories.

ILLUSTRATION

The best thing about our industry is the ability to switch gears, find a new tool, explore it and develop a new style. Our expertise and creativity go beyond 3D Animation. We strive to express our creativity and style in everything we do. Our Motion graphics with all the infographics and vector driven spots provides fresh animation styles to our work. They also give our clients something new to react to and enjoy. Our talented crews are here to serve your story, creating memorable titles, characters and design while focusing on every phase and frame of the production.

MOTION GRAPHICS

We are experts at motion design, integrating the elements of time and space to your otherwise static graphic elements to infuse the concept of movement. This ensures that your stories are paced and precise, communicating the required elements in time, to create an engaging message.

Our Motion graphics productions also creatively employ infographics and vector driven spots, providing a unique outlook to our motion graphics work.
